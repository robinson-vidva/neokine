## 1. What neokine is

neokine is a **visualization demo**. It runs pose estimation (MediaPipe) in your
browser to draw a skeleton over an image, video, or webcam feed and to plot
simple kinematics (displacement, velocity, joint angles, and a left–right
asymmetry index).
